PRIVACY POLICY — UK TAX ENGINE Effective Date: [30/4/2026] 1. INTRODUCTION This privacy policy describes how the UK Tax Engine mobile and web application ("the App") handles information. The App is designed with privacy as a core principle: it does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third party. 2. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT The App does not: • Require sign-up, registration, or login • Collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information • Collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or location data • Use third-party analytics services • Display advertisements • Track usage across other apps or websites • Share any data with third parties 3. INFORMATION STORED ON YOUR DEVICE The App stores the values you enter (such as salary, rental income, expenses) in your device's local storage so that you don't lose your inputs between sessions. This information: • Remains entirely on your device • Is not transmitted to any server • Is not accessible to the developer • Can be cleared at any time using the Reset button within the App • Is automatically removed if you uninstall the App 4.

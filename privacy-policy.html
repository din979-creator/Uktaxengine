Privacy Policy

App: UK Tax App

Last updated: 28 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how UK Tax App (“the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App is designed to help users estimate or understand UK tax-related information. You may enter information such as income, tax year, allowances, pension contributions, student loan details, or other tax-related figures.

Unless clearly stated otherwise inside the App, this information is processed locally on your device and is not sent to our servers.

Personal Information

We do not require you to create an account and we do not intentionally collect directly identifying personal information such as your name, address, phone number, National Insurance number, or HMRC login details.

How We Use Information

Information entered into the App is used to provide tax calculations, estimates, summaries, or related app functionality.

The App does not provide official tax, legal, financial, or accounting advice.

Analytics, Advertising, and Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services provided by Google Play or the Android operating system for app distribution, security, diagnostics, crash reporting, or usage analytics. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share tax information entered into the App with HMRC, employers, advertisers, or other third parties unless clearly disclosed and required for a feature you choose to use.

Data Storage and Security

Any information saved by the App is stored on your device unless the App clearly states otherwise. You can remove locally stored app data by using the App’s settings, clearing the App’s data in Android settings, or uninstalling the App.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

If applicable privacy laws give you rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal information, you may contact us using the details below.

Disclaimer

The App provides estimates and general information only. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) or the UK Government. You should check official HMRC guidance or seek professional advice before making tax decisions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
